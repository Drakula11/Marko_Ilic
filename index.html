About

Marko Ilić is an experienced water polo coach with a strong background in training youth athletes, both in the U.S. and Europe. As head coach of the 18U teams at Channel Islands United (2024–2025), he focuses on building solid technical foundations, team tactics, and a culture of respect and growth.

He has coached at elite-level camps across Texas and California, working with players aged 14–18, and has also led water polo and swimming programs in Serbia, including organizing camps for kids as young as six.

In addition to team coaching, Marko works as a personal coach, helping individuals of all ages improve fitness, technique, and confidence in the water. Whether it’s overcoming fear, refining posture, or preparing for high-level performance, his coaching is personalized, results-driven, and deeply supportive.

Marko holds a degree from the Faculty of Sports and Physical Education in Belgrade, where he also served as a ski demonstrator, lifeguard, and research assistant. His hands-on leadership and passion for working with young athletes make him a trusted mentor for developing both skill and character.
